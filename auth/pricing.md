# Faable Auth: Pricing 💳

Faable Auth is billed based on your **Monthly Active Users (MAU)**.


### 🆓 Community (Free)

Perfect for hobbyists and early-stage development.

- **Price**: 0€ / month
- **Included MAU**: up to 5,000
- **Passwordless**: Limited to a total of **100 emails** (absolute lifetime limit).
- **Features**: Social Login, Basic RBAC.

### 💎 Professional (Paid)

A comprehensive plan for growing applications and enterprises.

- **Base Price**: 99€ / month
- **Included MAU**: First 10,000 included
- **Overage**: 0.012€ per additional MAU.
- **Passwordless**: Full access to OTP & Magic Links (included within MAU).
- **Enterprise Features**: Custom Domain, MFA, Enterprise SSO / SAML.

> [!TIP]
> Our Professional plan is designed to be **40% more affordable** than comparable market leaders like Auth0, while providing the same enterprise-grade security.
